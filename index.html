The Sound of a Rain-Soaked Tuesday.

Gravité Fresq is a satirical post-punk project born from the gloom of Ireland. We exist at the intersection of 80s synthpop nostalgia and the crushing absurdity of modern corporate life.

We don’t just write songs; we paint sonic frescoes—layered, aggressive, and absurd musical landscapes designed for the cynics, the quiet quitters, and anyone who has ever stared into a cold purse and felt the void staring back.

The Sound

Our music fuses the mechanical precision of new wave with the raw, emotional exhaustion of post-punk. Think driving basslines, icy synthesizers, and melodies that stick in your head like a bad memo from HR. It is the sound of serious art colliding with dark, biting humor.

The Mission

We are here to dismantle the "Festive Season" and reclaim reality. Our lyrics tackle everything from the performative joy of office parties ("Festive Season My Arse") to the surreal debates that only happen in Irish pubs ("The Great Nipple Debate").

Gravité Fresq is the antidote to forced cheer. Join the rebellion.
